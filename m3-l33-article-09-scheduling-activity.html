📌 What This Activity Practiced

The Skills You Just Used

Reading AI flags as starting points, not instructions — every flag required you to think about what the AI knew and what it didn't before deciding how to respond.

Applying contextual knowledge the AI lacks — patient circumstances, appointment type nuances, operational factors specific to this week — to arrive at better decisions than the AI's data alone could produce.

Recognizing that both blind approval and reflexive rejection of AI recommendations are failures. The goal is informed judgment that combines AI data with professional knowledge.
